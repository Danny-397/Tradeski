Features

What Tradeski includes

01 — CHARTS
Real-Time Price Charts

Candlestick and line charts with live data across six timeframes — 1D through 1Y. Click any candle to inspect exact OHLC data in the strip below.

CANDLESTICK LINE 1D — 1Y
02 — INDICATORS
Technical Indicators

RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, SMA 20/50, EMA 20, Z-Score, ATR, and linear regression — computed server-side from first principles, overlaid on the chart.

RSI MACD BB SMA / EMA
03 — MACRO
FRED Macro Ribbon

CPI YoY%, Fed Funds Rate, GDP, Unemployment, 10-Year Treasury, Yield Curve spread, and High Yield credit spreads — pulled hourly from the St. Louis Fed.

CPI FED RATE 10Y YIELD HY SPREAD
04 — CORRELATION
Correlation Heatmap

90-day pairwise return correlation matrix across 11 symbols, rendered as a Plotly heatmap. Spot diversification gaps at a glance.

90-DAY RETURNS 11 SYMBOLS
05 — PORTFOLIO
Portfolio + Risk Metrics

Track holdings with live P&L. Sharpe ratio, beta vs SPY, and annualized volatility computed from one year of daily returns, weighted by market value.

SHARPE BETA VOLATILITY
06 — SKI
Ski — AI Financial Assistant

Ask Ski about markets, indicators, or your portfolio. Powered by Anthropic Claude with your live macro snapshot, holdings, and recent news injected as context.

CLAUDE MACRO CONTEXT PORTFOLIO AWARE
